iPhone 15 Pro Max Review: Apple's Best iPhone Yet

By John Smith
June 20, 2023
Smartphones
4.8 /5
Excellent

Our Verdict

The iPhone 15 Pro Max takes everything we loved about the iPhone 14 Pro Max and improves upon it in meaningful ways. With the new A17 Pro chip, titanium frame, and improved camera system, it's the most capable iPhone ever made. While it comes at a premium price, the performance, camera capabilities, and overall experience justify the cost for those who want the best smartphone available.

Pros

  • Incredibly powerful A17 Pro chip
  • Stunning camera system with 5x optical zoom
  • Beautiful and bright display
  • Premium titanium design
  • Excellent battery life

Cons

  • Very expensive
  • Still uses Lightning port
  • Heavy compared to non-Pro models
  • Charging speed could be faster

Introduction

Apple's iPhone 15 Pro Max represents the pinnacle of what the company can deliver in a smartphone in 2023. Building on the success of the iPhone 14 Pro Max, the new model introduces several key improvements including a more powerful A17 Pro chip, a redesigned titanium frame that reduces weight while maintaining durability, and significant camera upgrades.

Starting at $1,099 for the 128GB model, the iPhone 15 Pro Max sits at the premium end of the smartphone market. But does it justify its high price tag? After spending two weeks with the device, we're ready to share our comprehensive review.

Design and Build Quality

The iPhone 15 Pro Max features a premium titanium design with a frosted glass back

The iPhone 15 Pro Max introduces a titanium frame, replacing the stainless steel used in previous Pro models. This results in a device that's noticeably lighter (about 19g less than the 14 Pro Max) while maintaining the premium feel and durability expected from Apple's flagship device.

The matte glass back and slightly curved edges make the phone comfortable to hold despite its large size. The Dynamic Island introduced in the iPhone 14 Pro series returns, continuing to serve as both a functional and aesthetic element of the display.

The device is available in four colors: Natural Titanium, Blue Titanium, White Titanium, and Black Titanium. Our review unit is the Blue Titanium, which has a subtle, sophisticated look that's distinctly premium without being flashy.

Apple has maintained the IP68 water and dust resistance rating, allowing the device to withstand submersion in up to 6 meters of water for 30 minutes. The Ceramic Shield front continues to offer excellent drop protection.

Display

The iPhone 15 Pro Max features a 6.7-inch Super Retina XDR display with ProMotion technology, supporting refresh rates from 1Hz to 120Hz. The screen can reach a peak brightness of 2,000 nits outdoors and 1,600 nits when viewing HDR content.

Colors are vibrant and accurate, with the OLED panel delivering deep blacks and excellent contrast. The ProMotion display continues to be one of the best in the business, with smooth scrolling and responsive interactions.

The always-on display introduced in the iPhone 14 Pro returns, showing the time, date, and notifications at a glance without significantly impacting battery life.

Performance

Technical Specifications

Processor A17 Pro (3nm)
RAM 8GB
Storage 128GB/256GB/512GB/1TB
Operating System iOS 17

The A17 Pro chip represents a significant leap forward in mobile processing power. Built on a 3nm process, it delivers approximately 20% better CPU performance and 30% better GPU performance compared to the A16 Bionic in the iPhone 14 Pro Max.

In our benchmark tests, the iPhone 15 Pro Max consistently outperformed all Android competitors and its predecessor. Day-to-day tasks like app launching, multitasking, and web browsing feel instantaneous, while demanding games run at high frame rates with no signs of thermal throttling even during extended sessions.

Apple has increased the RAM to 8GB (up from 6GB in the previous generation), which further enhances multitasking capabilities and future-proofs the device for upcoming iOS features and updates.

Camera samples showing the versatility of the iPhone 15 Pro Max camera system

The camera system is where the iPhone 15 Pro Max truly shines. The main 48MP camera can now capture 48MP ProRAW photos with incredible detail, and the new 5x optical zoom telephoto lens (exclusive to the Pro Max model) provides much better zoom capabilities than previous iPhones.

Low-light performance has improved across all three rear cameras, with the Night mode activating more quickly and delivering cleaner results with less noise. Portrait mode photos benefit from better depth mapping and more natural bokeh effects.

Video capabilities continue to impress, with the ability to shoot 4K60 HDR video across all cameras, including the front-facing camera. The new Cinematic 4K/60fps mode and improved image stabilization make this the best smartphone for video recording on the market.

Apple has also introduced a new Photographic Styles feature that allows for more personalized color tuning without looking like a heavy-handed filter.

Battery Life and Charging

The iPhone 15 Pro Max continues the excellent battery life tradition established by the 14 Pro Max. In our typical day of testing—which includes web browsing, social media, video streaming, photography, and some gaming—the phone easily lasted from morning to night with 20-30% remaining.

Heavy users will still get through a full day, while moderate users might stretch to two days between charges. This impressive endurance comes despite the more powerful processor and brighter display.

Charging speeds remain largely unchanged, with the phone supporting 20W wired charging (reaching about 50% in 30 minutes) and 15W MagSafe wireless charging. While not the fastest charging speeds in the premium smartphone segment, they're adequate for most users.

Software: iOS 17

The iPhone 15 Pro Max ships with iOS 17, which introduces several new features and refinements. Standout additions include interactive widgets, a new StandBy mode when charging in landscape orientation, and improved privacy controls.

The Dynamic Island continues to evolve with iOS 17, now supporting more apps and functions. It remains one of the most elegant solutions to housing the front camera and Face ID sensors while adding functional value.

As always with Apple, software support will be excellent, with the iPhone 15 Pro Max likely to receive updates for at least 5-6 years.

Conclusion

The iPhone 15 Pro Max is undoubtedly the best iPhone Apple has ever made, and one of the best smartphones available today. The combination of the powerful A17 Pro chip, versatile camera system with 5x optical zoom, premium titanium design, and excellent battery life creates a compelling package for those who want a no-compromise smartphone experience.

While the high price will deter some potential buyers, those who can afford it will be rewarded with a device that excels in virtually every area and should remain competitive for years to come. If you're looking for the best of what Apple has to offer and don't mind paying a premium, the iPhone 15 Pro Max is the phone to get.

Final Score: 4.8/5
